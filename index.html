Rise of Harry Potter

J.K. Rowling is the author of the record-breaking,multi-award-wining Harry Potter novels. Loved by fans around the world,the series has sold over 500 million copies, been translated into 80 languages, made into eight blockbuster films. She has written three companian volumes in aid of charity: Qudditch Through the ages and Fantastic beasts and where to find them (in aid of comic relief and lumos),and the tales of bettle the bard (in aid of lumos), as well as a screenplay inspired by Fantastic beasts and where to find them, which marked the start of five-film series to be written by the author. She has also collaborated on a stage play, Harry Potter and the Cursed Child Parts One and Two, which opned in London's west End in the summer of 2016 and on Brodway in the early 2018. In 2012, J.K. Rowling's digital company Pottermore was launched, where fans can enjoy news,features and articles, as well as orignal content from J.K. Rowling. She has received many awards and honours, including an OBE and Companian of Honour, France's Legion d'honneur, and the Hans Christian Aneresen Award.
